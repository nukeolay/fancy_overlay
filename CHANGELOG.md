## 0.1.2

* Added `LiquidDistortionOverlay` with Mild, Balanced, and Heavy presets.
* Added one-pass Liquid Glass backdrop refraction with organic procedural flow,
  optional chromatic separation, shader precaching, reduced-motion support, and
  a deterministic portable caustic fallback.

## 0.1.1

* Improved `VhsOverlay` with a rounded-rectangular CRT vignette for a more authentic appearance.

## 0.1.0

* Initial release with customizable Flutter overlay effects:
  * `FallingWidgetsOverlay`
  * `VhsOverlay`
  * `PixelNoiseOverlay`
  * `PixelizeOverlay`
  * `VignetteOverlay`
* `BuildContext` extension methods for showing and removing overlays.
